About me

I was born in Lagos, Nigeria almost three decades ago. From an early age I showed interest for computers and that grew into a passion during high school. I got into web design and development in college. My interest for computers was the best decision I ever made because it showed me what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. Currently I help businesses all around the world by designing and coding websites and mobile apps. I had the pleasure of working with companies like Medessy Enterprises Ltd, LaSavannah, just to name a few. Currently now I’m based in Lagos.
